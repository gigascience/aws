Information

SmileFinder is a tool for detecting diversity and divergence patterns associated with selection sweeps by evaluating allele frequencies. The collection of SmileFinder scripts have been deployed as tools in GigaGalaxy and can be used within a workflow to identify a difference in diversity between two African populations.
